5 Tips to Start Your Trading Journey

Starting your trading journey can be overwhelming, but these five tips will help you build a solid foundation...

To begin trading:

  1. Understand trading basics: stocks, forex, and cryptocurrencies.
  2. Choose a strategy: day trading or long-term investing.
  3. Open a demo account and practice risk-free.
  4. Master risk management with stop-loss orders.
  5. Stay informed about the market trends and news.
